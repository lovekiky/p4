ART BRAND LOGO


FREELANCE, 2022


ILLUSTRATOR, PROCREATE

During my gap year, I started a clothing brand with a goal of making wearable art that could help shift one’s mindset and mental health in a positive direction. I designed the logo to look like a butterfly--the Greek’s symbol for the psyche--and subtly included the letters of the brand’s name. I used jagged corners (as opposed to rounded) to prevent the logo from looking too feminine because I wanted the brand to be unisex.
