FESTIVAL BRANDING


TYPOGRAPHY, 2023


ILLUSTRATOR, PROCREATE

WORDMARK, BRANDING, POSTER, TICKET, PRODUCTS, SOCIAL MEDIA, NAME TAG, & BROCHURE

Frosting Fiesta is an annual, bilingual confectionary festival held in LA, California every spring. It celebrates a love for sweets and parties, and is welcoming to all! This project started out by creating a wordmark for a specific festival using a brand identity guide. Following with the style of the wordmark, I then created a branding package with a color palette, fonts, patterns, and the deliverables listed above.
