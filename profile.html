HELLO!

KIANA LIN

SHE/HER






KIANA

is a graphic designer, screenprinter, and artist based in central Illinois. Graphic design has become an exciting interest to her as it combines the elements of creativity with problem solving: a perfect pair one might say. She is motivated to use her artistic skills to create solutions that can help others and make life more beautiful.



SHE LOVES

to dabble in many forms of art! Sewing, yarnwork, charcoal, and sculpture to name a few. She also enjoys finding new music and eating delicious food. More than her hobbies, she is a big advocate for personal growth through body and mind. Her biggest inspirations are from nature and the mundane. Beauty is everywhere.

"Art is a form of expression--it can speak where one lacks eloquence. I strive to create art that can connect, create community, and inspire growth."
